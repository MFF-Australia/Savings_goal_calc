Description

This tool calculates the regular deposits required to achieve your savings goal.

Assumptions

  • Account fees are not considered.
  • Income tax on earned interest is not considered.
  • The interest rate remains constant throughout the saving term.
  • Interest is compounded at the same frequency as deposits (weekly, fortnightly, monthly). In reality, compounding may differ from the deposit frequency.
  • A year is assumed to have 26 fortnights or 52 weeks, totaling 364 days, instead of 365 or 366 days.
  • No rounding is applied during the calculation, though account balances are typically rounded to at least the nearest cent in practice.
